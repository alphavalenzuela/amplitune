Biography

Hailing from the scenic town of Bacoor, Cavite, Kayl Garcia is a gifted singer-songwriter whose melodious tunes and heartfelt lyrics have captured the hearts of listeners far and wide. Born with a natural inclination towards music, Kayl embarked on her musical journey in 2013, setting out on a mission to inspire others to embrace their passion for music and self-expression.

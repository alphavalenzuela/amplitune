Biography

After winning "Wanderband," in 2014, a competition between the Philippines emerging young acts, the Ransom Collective swiftly found themselves on stages all over Manila. The six-piece indie folk group went on to play the annual Wanderland Festival, sharing the stage with bands such as The Drums, The Royal Concept, and Architecture in Helsinki. The Ransom Collective then released their self-titled EP in November 2014, and their full length album "Traces" in May 2017, both to high praise from music critics and peers.
